Grizzly Bear

While preparing to hibernate, a Grizzly bear can eat up to 90 lbs of food to gain over 2 lbs of body weight a day.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus arctos horribilis
  • Average Weight: 800 lbs
  • Average Height: 8 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 30 years in the wild
  • Habitat: Dense forest in North America

Grizzly bears are highly intelligent creatures and are able to find food from long distances away due to its sense of smell. Female Grizzlies will actually give birth while hibernating and the cubs will nurse until spring.
